The Solar System

Introduction

The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, including planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. Our Solar System is located in the Milky Way galaxy and has been the focus of space exploration.

Planets in the Solar System

  • Mercury - The smallest planet and the closest to the Sun.
  • Venus - Known for its extreme temperatures and thick atmosphere.
  • Earth - The only planet known to support life.
